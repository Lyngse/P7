Tasovice is a village and municipality (obec) in Blansko District in the South Moravian Region of the Czech Republic.

The municipality covers an area of 3.27 square kilometres (1.26 sq mi), and has a population of 67 (as at 3 July 2006).

Tasovice lies approximately 21 kilometres (13 mi) north-west of Blansko, 36 km (22 mi) north of Brno, and 160 km (99 mi) south-east of Prague.
